EVENT STRUCTURE


Introduction

  • Gear Up for the Ultimate Robot Battle! Get ready to witness an adrenaline-pumping clash of metal, might, and mind-blowing technology! Brace yourself as fierce, battle-hardened robots from around the globe collide in an arena of chaos and destruction. This is no ordinary showdown — it’s Robowars at Tekron 2025! Watch sparks fly as these steel titans unleash raw power, strategy, and unyielding force in a ruthless arena designed for only the strongest to survive. No mercy. No surrender. Only victory. If you’re a true warrior at heart, this is your call to arms! Join thousands of robo-maniacs and experience the thrill of the century. Witness the fiercest battles where every hit counts, every move matters, and every victory is earned, not given. Will your creation be the last bot standing? It's time to claim your legacy in the wildest robotic warzone ever created. Be There. Witness History. Fight for Glory. See you at Tekron 2025.

Eligibility

  • Eligibility for Participation: All students possessing a valid identity card from their respective educational institute are eligible to participate.
  • Certificate of Excellence: The top two teams in each competition category will be awarded a Certificate of Excellence and respective prize(s).
  • Certificate of Participation: All teams that qualify for Round 1 will receive a Certificate of Participation.
  • Disqualification: Teams that are disqualified at any stage will not be eligible for any certificates.

Team Specifications

  • Team Size: Maximum of 6 members.
  • Team Composition: All members must be from the same educational institute.
  • Team Name: Unique names required; offensive names may be rejected.
  • Team ID: Each team will receive a unique ID for communication.

Certification Policy

  • Compliance with Rules: Mandatory adherence to event rules at all times.
  • Battery and Pneumatic Safety: Special care must be taken to protect onboard batteries and pneumatic systems. Robots lacking proper protection will be disqualified.
  • Innovative Designs: Unique designs must seek approval from organizers prior to competition.
  • Safety Inspections: All robots will undergo safety inspections before competition clearance.
  • Activation and Deactivation: Robots should only be activated in specified areas.
  • Weapon Safety: Sharp-edged weapons must have protective covers when not in use.
  • General Safety Guidelines: Participants operate robots at their own risk; safety measures must be followed to avoid injuries.

Portfolio Submission

  • Requirements: Comprehensive portfolio including a written abstract, images, CAD models, or videos of the robot.
  • Submission Format: Must adhere to specified format; late submissions will not be considered.
  • Eligibility: Only teams that clear the shortlisting process will participate.
  • No human interference (touching the robot during the game)

Gameplay Structure

  • Match Duration: Each match lasts for 3 minutes.
  • Arena Regulations: Robots must not damage the arena; penalties apply for violations.
  • Knock-out Format: Robots must demonstrate linear motion within 30 seconds to avoid being knocked out.

Bot Dimensions and Fabrication

  • Size Restrictions:- No limitations on the dimensions of the robot(s).
  • Weight Category :- 8 kg (with a tolerance of ±5%)
  • Onboard Components and Weight Calculation :-
    • Pneumatic tanks and batteries must be part of the robot's onboard structure.
    • The weight of the remote control system is not included in the total weight calculation.
    • The weight of the battery is included in the total weight of the robot.

Weapon Systems

  • Permissible Weapons: Includes cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices, and spinning hammers.
  • Prohibited Weapons: Includes liquid projectiles, flame-based weapons, explosives, and entanglement devices.
  • Weapon Safety: Spinning weapons must cease spinning within 60 seconds of power loss.

Hydraulics and Pneumatics

  • Fluid Requirements: Only non-flammable and non-corrosive fluids allowed.
  • Pressure Limitations: Maximum permissible pressure is 50 bars.
  • Component Security: All components must be securely mounted.

Mobility

  • Permitted Mobility Methods: Rolling and walking mechanisms are allowed.
  • Prohibited Mobility Methods: Flying mechanisms and uncontrolled aerial maneuvers are not allowed.

Robot Control Requirements

  • Control Type: Only wireless remote controls are allowed.
  • Emergency Stop: Must include an emergency stop system.

Design Specifications

  • Power Source: Robots must be powered solely by electricity.
  • Battery Requirements: Sealed, immobilized-electrolyte batteries are required.

Arena Specifications

  • Dimensions : 12ft x 12ft x 6ft (l x b x h).
  • 2 * 6 m ( shree metal ) . 6 * 19.5 210/kg
  • Safety Precautions: The arena is protected by bulletproof polycarbonate walls of 10mm thickness at four sides (excluding the top).
  • The top is protected by a 6mm bulletproof polycarbonate sheet.
  • Arena Diagram: The out-to-out dimension of the arena will be 12ft x 12ft x 8ft (l x b x h). As well as the arena might be functional. The arena size is also subject to the infrastructure. Polycarbonate thickness may be increased.

Scoring Criteria

  • Aggression: Frequency and effectiveness of attacks.
  • Control: Ability to attack opponents effectively while minimizing damage.
  • Damage: Direct or indirect reduction of opponent functionality.
  • Strategy: Combat plans that exploit strengths and weaknesses.

Important Notes

  • Final Authority: Decisions made by organizers and judges are final.
  • Misconduct & Violations: Immediate disqualification for rule violations.

Important Dates & Deadlines

  • Registration Deadline: February 26, 2025 (11:59 PM IST)
  • Video Submission Deadline: February 28, 2025 (11:59 PM IST)
  • Event Day: March 5, 2025 (9:30 AM IST)
  • Finals: March 5, 2025 (10:30 AM IST)
