DRONE HAVOC

RULES

INTRODUCTION

The Drone Havoc is a fast-paced sport where pilots control high-speed drones through challenging courses filled with obstacles and sharp turns.

Prize Pool : ₹100000

Swiftest Drone (Fastest Lap Completion)

  • 1st Place : ₹30,000
  • 2nd Place : ₹20,000

AeroDodger (Most Hoops Crossed)

  • 1st Place : ₹30,000
  • 2nd Place : ₹20,000

Eligibility:

  • Open to students of Class 11/12 and UG/PG programs.
  • Teams of up to 4 members.
  • Registration deadline: March 2

Event Flow

  • Teams check-in, receive guidelines, and undergo drone inspection.
  • Introduction to rules, track, and scoring.
  • Race execution and score tallying.
  • Winners announced, followed by prize distribution and closing remarks.

Event Details

  • Custom-made drones only (no DJI or professional drones).
  • FPV Goggles not allowed.
  • No restrictions on controllers.
  • Extra batteries recommended.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants must carry ID cards.
  • Reach venue 30 minutes early.
  • Drones start from the launchpad; timer begins at takeoff.
  • Checkpoints available for repositioning after crashes.
  • Only *pilot and co-pilot* can handle the drone during the race.
  • Moving with the drone is allowed.
  • If a drone is inactive after a crash, time continues running.
  • Placing the drone incorrectly or external interference results in disqualification.

Judging Criteria

  • Swiftest Drone : Shortest lap time.
  • AeroDodger : Maximum hoops crossed within the time limit.
