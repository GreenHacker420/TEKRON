CodeWars

Overview

CodeWars is a competitive programming contest held as part of Tekron. It will consist of two rounds: an online qualification round and an offline final round. Participants will compete in algorithmic problem-solving to test their coding skills and logical thinking.

Contest Format

Round 1: Online Qualification Round

  • Date: February 26th, 2025
  • Platform: Newton School Portal
  • Format: Individual competition
  • Selection Criteria: A set number of top performers will be shortlisted for the next round (number not disclosed)
  • Time:5:00pm-8:00pm

Round 2: Offline Round

  • Date: March 6th, 2025
  • Venue: Newton School of Technology
  • Platform: Newton School Portal (Proctored environment with screen, video, and audio monitoring)
  • Format: Individual competition
  • Time: 2:00pm-5:00pm
  • Requirement: Participants must bring their own laptops
  • Duration: 3 hours

Rules and Regulations:

  • Any form of plagiarism or unfair means will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The contest will follow the standard rules of the ICPC Format.
  • The offline round will be conducted on the Newton School portal with strict proctoring, including screen, video, and audio monitoring
  • The decision of the organizing team will be final in case of disputes.

Judging Criteria

ICPC Style: You will be ranked based on the number of problems solved, and ties will be broken based on penalty.

Registration Details

  • Registration Link: on Unstop or Our Website
  • Deadline: 26/02/25-3:00pm
  • Fee: None
