ABOUT US

Welcome to TEKRON, an electrifying celebration of technology,innovation, and creativity! This fest brings together tech enthusiasts, problem solvers, and curious minds for a series of thrilling challenges designed to test skills, foster collaboration, and push boundaries.

Whether you're coding, building, strategizing, or solving puzzles, TEKRON offers an immersive experience filled with excitement and discovery.

Connect with like-minded individuals, showcase your talents, and take on challenges that will keep you engaged from start to finish.

Get ready for an unforgettable journey where ideas come to life and the future of technology takes center stage.

Let the games begin!
